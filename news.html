News

New Collection: "Harmony in Motion"

March 15, 2024

I'm excited to announce my latest collection, "Harmony in Motion," which explores the relationship between movement and stillness in textile art. This series features twelve new pieces that incorporate experimental stitching techniques and natural dye processes.

The collection will be featured in an upcoming group exhibition at the Contemporary Textile Arts Gallery this spring. More details to follow.

Workshop Series: Introduction to Textile Art

February 28, 2024

I'll be leading a series of workshops this summer focusing on traditional and contemporary textile techniques. These hands-on sessions are designed for both beginners and experienced practitioners looking to expand their skills.

Topics will include embroidery fundamentals, natural dyeing, and mixed-media approaches to textile art. Registration opens next month.

Artist Residency Program

January 10, 2024

I'm honored to have been selected for a three-month artist residency program at the Textile Arts Center. This opportunity will allow me to develop new work and collaborate with other artists in the field.

During the residency, I'll be exploring large-scale installations and experimenting with sustainable materials. I'll be sharing updates throughout the process.

Featured in Textile Arts Quarterly

December 5, 2023

My recent work was featured in the winter issue of Textile Arts Quarterly, including an interview about my creative process and artistic influences. The article includes beautiful photography of several pieces from my recent collections.

Copies are available through the publication's website or at select art bookstores.

Gallery Opening: "Urban Reflections"

November 20, 2023

The opening reception for my solo exhibition "Urban Reflections" was a wonderful success. Thank you to everyone who attended and shared their thoughts on the work.

The exhibition will remain on view through the end of the month. If you haven't had a chance to visit yet, I encourage you to stop by.
