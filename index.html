Welcome

Welcome to the artistic world of Ingrid Aszkenasy. Through a unique blend of traditional techniques and contemporary vision, my work explores the intersection of texture, color, and form.

Each piece tells a story, capturing moments of beauty and introspection through the medium of textile art and mixed media.
